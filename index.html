The Obstacles Of College life vary for everybody. From keeping up with academics to controlling your finances and juggling your job schedule. Moving away from family, adapting to being financial stable and maintaining a healthy social life.

Adjusting your mind to your major

One of the biggest problems that freshman encounter during college classroom experience was failing to communicate with their peers. No matter the major, from Dentistry to Marketing, communication is either mandatory or strongly recommended for all of the majors in the colligiate field.

Molding your mind to your major through constant communication with your peers is a lacking skill that's essential for your success and if it's not worked on then it can lead onto bad habits like procrastination.
